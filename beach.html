Goose Creek Beach

Spring Into Summer!

Goose Creek Beach officially opens to the public on Friday, May 26

Permit Type Price
Resident parking permit $30 / vehicle
Resident Guest parking permit (non-residents) $40 / vehicle
Non-Resident parking permit $150 / vehicle
Where to Purchase Permits may be purchased at the Town Clerk's Office from 8 a.m. to 4 p.m. They are also available at Town Beach, Norman E. Klipp Park, or New Suffolk Beach from beach attendants from 10 a.m. to 3 p.m.

Getting There

Address North Bayview Road, Southold, NY 11971

Directions Take Main Rd / NY-25 East. Turn right onto Main Bayview Road in Southold. Take the first left onto Pine Neck Road. Turn right onto N. Bayview Road and continue about half a mile. Goose Creek Beach will be on the left.
