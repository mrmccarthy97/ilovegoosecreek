Experience Goose Creek

Enjoy the exquisite views of Goose Creek right in your own backyard, or spend a day at Goose Creek Beach overlooking the Southold Bay.

Located at the head of Goose Creek, this is a popular spot for beachgoers and kayakers alike. The calm water makes it perfect for exploring by kayak. Afterward, return to the beach and spend the day relaxing with family and friends. The beach has areas to pull up your boat or stake out your spot in the sand.
